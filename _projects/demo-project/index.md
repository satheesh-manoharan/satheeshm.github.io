## Overview
The platform combined modern data pipelines with semantic modeling so non-technical stakeholders could explore KPIs and business trends independently.

## What I delivered
- Designed ETL/ELT pipelines that moved data from source systems into curated analytics datasets.
- Built reusable dbt models and metric definitions for consistent KPI reporting.
- Developed Apache Superset dashboards for operational and executive teams.
- Partnered with product and analytics stakeholders to shape an experience that scaled across business domains.

## Impact
The solution improved self-service analytics adoption and reduced the dependency on engineering teams for routine reporting requests.
